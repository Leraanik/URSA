Land Freight

At URSA we understand that efficient land transportation is the backbone of a strong supply chain. We offer a robust portfolio of land freight services designed to get your cargo where it needs to be, on time and within budget.

Here’s why you can rely on URSA for your land freight needs:

  • 01

    Extensive coverage

    Our expansive network of trusted carriers ensures we can handle shipments across short and long distances, regionally or nationwide.

  • 02

    Diverse fleet options

    We offer a variety of truck types and trailers to accommodate any size or type of cargo, from standard pallets to oversized machinery.

  • 03

    Cost-effective solutions

    We work tirelessly to optimize routes and leverage our buying power to secure competitive rates, ensuring your land freight is cost-efficient.

  • 04

    On-time delivery

    We prioritize reliable scheduling and efficient route planning to guarantee your cargo arrives at its destination on time, every time.

  • 05

    Advanced tracking

    Our advanced tracking technology provides real-time visibility of your shipment’s location throughout its journey.

  • 06

    Dedicated account management

    A dedicated account manager will be your point of contact, ensuring clear communication and personalized service throughout the shipping process.

Partner with URSA for your land freight needs and experience the power of a secure, reliable, and cost-effective transportation solution.
